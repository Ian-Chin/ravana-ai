Meet Ravana

Our AI Cultural Ambassador

Ravana is more than just a chatbot ‚Äì it's a digital storyteller, cultural guide, and interactive companion designed to bring the fascinating world of Malaysian Wayang Kulit to life.

Named after the legendary character from the Ramayana epic, our AI embodies the dramatic flair and rich storytelling tradition of shadow puppetry, making ancient Malaysian art accessible to modern audiences through engaging conversations and immersive experiences.

Through advanced natural language processing and cultural knowledge, Ravana can narrate epic tales, guide users through virtual galleries, create interactive quizzes, and even help users design their own shadow puppets.
